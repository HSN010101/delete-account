Delete Your LangCafe Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request the deletion of your LangCafe account and all associated data by following the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account:

  1. Using the App:
    1. Open LangCafe App: Launch the app on your device.
    2. Go to Settings: Navigate to the account settings or profile section.
    3. Delete Account: Click on the "Delete Account" button and follow the prompts.
  2. Using the Web:
    1. Email Request: Send an email to languagescafes@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My LangCafe Account".
    2. Provide Details: Include your account email and any other required information in the email.

What Happens to Your Data:

Upon deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User authentication information (email, phone number)
  • Posts and content you have created
  • Voice room data

Some data may be retained in our backups for up to 30 days for recovery purposes.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions about deleting your account, please contact us at languagescafes@gmail.com.
